Privacy Policy

What information VersionDB collects and how it's stored

1. Information We Collect

We only collect email addresses in two situations:

  1. Payments: When you purchase a dataset, your email is collected by Stripe to send payment confirmations and receipts. We do not store your payment information ourselves.
  2. Direct Contact: If you email us for any reason—questions, support, or arranging payment in crypto—we store the email you used to contact us.

We do not collect any other personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking beyond what is needed to process payments or respond to emails.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to deliver our service:

  • Stripe: For secure processing of payments. Your email is shared with Stripe for receipts and payment confirmation. We do not store your payment details.

No other third-party service is used to collect or store your personal information.

3. Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking. Any cookies present are only for basic website functionality, if at all.

4. Security

While we do our best to keep any data we receive safe, email communication over the internet is never fully secure. By contacting us via email, you acknowledge this limitation.

5. Data Retention

We only keep your email as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry or to provide payment confirmation for your purchase.

6. No Selling of Data

We will never sell or share your personal data with third parties.

7. Changes to This Policy

If our practices change, we will update this Privacy Policy on our website.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about privacy, payments, or anything else, you can contact us via email: jesse@versiondb.io
